Patient Education

Know Your Health

Simple, important health information — because an informed patient heals better.

Know the Signs of Appendicitis

अपेंडिक्स के लक्षण जानें

Sudden pain starting around the navel moving to the lower right abdomen, fever, nausea — do not delay. This is a surgical emergency. Reach the nearest hospital immediately.

Piles Are Treatable

बवासीर का इलाज संभव है

Haemorrhoids (piles) are common and very treatable. Do not suffer in silence due to embarrassment. Modern surgical techniques mean minimal pain and fast recovery.

Cervical Cancer Screening

सर्वाइकल कैंसर जाँच ज़रूरी है

Every woman above 21 should have a regular Pap smear test. Cervical cancer is almost 100% preventable when detected early. The HPV vaccine is available — ask your doctor.

C-Section vs Normal Delivery

सिजेरियन बनाम सामान्य प्रसव

A C-section is a major surgery, not a shortcut. It is recommended only when medically necessary. Always ask your surgeon why it's being advised before agreeing.

Get a Second Opinion

दूसरी राय लेना आपका अधिकार है

Before any major surgery, you have every right to ask another doctor. A responsible surgeon will welcome this. It helps you feel confident and safe about your decision.

Gallstone Symptoms

पित्त की पथरी के लक्षण

Sudden severe pain in the upper right abdomen, especially after fatty meals, nausea, or yellowing of skin — these could be gallstones. Laparoscopic surgery offers quick, scar-free recovery.
